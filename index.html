Bio

I'm a Research Scientist at Featurespace with the Deep Learning Research Group. Previously, I was a research fellow at the University of St Andrews in geometric measure theory, funded by an early career fellowship from the London Mathematical Society and the Heilbronn Institute for Mathematical Research.
