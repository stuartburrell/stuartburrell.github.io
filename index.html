Bio

I'm a Senior Research Engineer at Featurespace, where we build real-time AI/ML systems for combating financial crime - a rising epidemic with links to organised crime, human trafficking, and even terrorism.

Before moving to industry, I was a research fellow at the University of St Andrews in geometric measure theory, funded by an early career fellowship from the London Mathematical Society and the Heilbronn Institute for Mathematical Research.
